Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy page for Shree Pathology Report Automator ("we", "our", or "the Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information processed through our Google Chrome Extension.

1. Single Purpose & Functionality

The Extension serves a strict, narrow single purpose: to automate, format, and customize the layout of digital pathology laboratory reports for authorized diagnostic lab operators. It helps users insert custom letterhead headers and pathologist signatures directly into patient report print previews.

2. Information We Collect and Process

To deliver its core functionality, the Extension handles data in the following transparent manner:

  • Laboratory Configuration Parameters: When initializing your workspace, you provide setup parameters including Laboratory Name, Subtitle, Establishment Registration ID, Address, Contact Number, and Pathologist Signatory Credentials (Doctor Name, Degree, and MCI/Registration Number).
  • Authentication State: We check your registered Gmail address against our remote database to validate account license activation states.
🔒 User Privacy Assurance (No Patient Data Collection):
The Extension strictly processes patient names and diagnostic test parameters locally inside your browser's context to generate print previews. We do not collect, read, harvest, store, or transmit any patient medical data or diagnostic test results to any external servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security Permissions

  • Storage Permission: Used solely to save your specific laboratory profile configuration locally in your browser's secure cache context. This prevents you from having to re-type lab details during every session.
  • Tabs Permission: Used strictly to isolate and open a clean browser tab to cleanly draw the print preview window layout (`window.print()`) without blocking your primary dashboard workspace.
  • Cloud Database (Firestore REST API): Used explicitly to match user account status (active/inactive) and save your custom lab layout mapping schema securely.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share any collected data with third-party networks, advertising companies, or analytics companies. All stored parameters are used strictly to run the extension features for your internal business usage.

5. Google Chrome Web Store Policy Compliance

This Extension complies fully with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use restrictions. We ensure narrow usage of data solely to improve and provide the report layout automation features described.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this web page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or data handling practices, please contact the administrator team at your registered support portal.
